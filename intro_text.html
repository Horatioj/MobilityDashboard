WELCOME TO Mobility Equity Dashboard

An interactive tool for visualizing mobility equities in United States

This tool enables intuitive, rapid and reproducible quality of mobility pattern analysis in Washtington D.C., New York City, and Boston. A transportation flow simulation is also included in the last section. The main work is based on: Routing in Mixed Transportation Systems for Mobility Equity & A Mobility Equity Metric for Multi-Modal Intelligent Transportation Systems. You can find more information from Cornell University Information and Decision Science Laboratory.

 CONTENTS IN THIS DASHBOARD:

  • An introductory page explaining: Mobility Index (MI) & Mobility Equity Metric (MEM).
  • Dynamic visualizations of MI @ community level and MEM @ city level in three cities. 
  • Transportation flow simulating travel time at different ratios of public transit @ Boston and its relationship with MEM.

Click top banners to start. You can find an instruction button in the DC section.
