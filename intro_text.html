WELCOME TO Mobility Equity Dashboard

An interactive tool for visualizing mobility equities in United States

This tool enables intuitive, rapid and reproducible quality of mobility pattern analysis in Washtington D.C., New York City, and Boston. A transportation network flow is also included. The main work is based on: Routing in Mixed Transportation Systems for Mobility Equity & A Mobility Equity Metric for Multi-Modal Intelligent Transportation Systems. You can find more information from Cornell University Information and Decision Science Laboratory.

 CONTENTS IN THIS DASHBOARD:

  • An introductory page explaining ideas: Mobility Index (MI) & Mobility Equity Metric (MEM).
  • Dynamic visualizations of MI @ community level and MEM @ city level. 
  • Dynamic transportation flow and its relationship with MEM from our transportation simulation models.

Data are mostly open-sourced, including American Community Survey, isolines API, and others.
